Who I Am

Over three decades in classrooms and corridors, I've worn many hats — Teacher, Vice Principal, Workshop Leader, and Curriculum Innovator. My journey began with roots in Roorkee's rich academic tradition and has evolved into a unique approach that bridges rigorous physics education with joyful, experiential learning.

I believe that physics isn't just about formulas and theories—it's about understanding the wonder that surrounds us every day. From the sound waves echoing through a forest to the optical phenomena during monsoons, I help educators and students discover the physics that happens to them naturally.

My work spans traditional classroom teaching, innovative curriculum development, teacher training programs, and educational satire that challenges conventional approaches. When I'm not teaching physics, I'm composing thoughts in music, humor, and ideas that spark genuine curiosity and reflective learning.

What I Offer

Experiential Learning Workshops

Hands-on physics workshops that transform abstract concepts into tangible experiences. I design sessions that connect classroom learning with real-world phenomena, making physics accessible and exciting for learners of all ages.

Educator Training & Mentoring

Comprehensive professional development programs for physics teachers and educators. I share practical strategies, innovative teaching methods, and reflective practices that transform how physics is taught and learned.

Nature-based Physics Modules

Unique curriculum modules that explore physics concepts through natural phenomena. From forest acoustics to monsoon optics, these programs foster both scientific understanding and environmental awareness.

Curriculum & CPD Consulting

Strategic consulting for educational institutions seeking to modernize their physics curriculum and continuous professional development programs. I help schools create more engaging and effective learning experiences.

Educational Satire Writing

Thought-provoking satirical content that challenges conventional educational practices. My writings offer humorous yet insightful perspectives on teaching, learning, and educational bureaucracy.

Creative Learning Integration

Innovative approaches that blend music, humor, and creativity with physics education. I help educators incorporate artistic elements to create more memorable and engaging learning experiences.
